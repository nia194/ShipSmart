# Restricted & Prohibited Items

ShipSmart knowledge base — advisory guidance; rules vary by carrier and destination.

## Commonly restricted categories

- **Batteries / power devices** — lithium cells and power banks (see the dangerous-goods
  guide); often restricted by mode and state of charge.
- **Liquids, aerosols, flammables** — perfumes, nail polish, lighters, spray cans; many
  are limited or air-prohibited.
- **Perishables** — food and plants face phytosanitary and shelf-life limits.
- **Hazmat** — paints, solvents, magnets, dry ice; require declarations.
- **Weapons & replicas** — firearms, ammunition, knives; broadly prohibited or licensed.
- **Currency, precious metals** — high-value monetary items are widely restricted.
- **Alcohol & tobacco** — licensed, taxed, and frequently prohibited by consumer carriers.

## Destination-specific rules

Restrictions are **per destination**, not just per item. The same item can ship freely to
one country and be controlled in another. Always check the importing country's rules in
addition to the carrier's.

### Electronics & radio-frequency devices

Many countries require **type approval / homologation** for devices that transmit radio
signals (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, cellular, drone control links) before they can be imported or
operated.

- **Brazil** — radio-transmitting products, including **drones (UAVs)**, require **ANATEL
  homologation**; drones also fall under ANAC and DECEA airspace rules. A drone shipped to
  Brazil therefore raises a destination-specific import-and-operation control beyond its
  lithium battery — uncertified units can be held or refused at customs.
- **India** — many drones require DGCA clearance and an import license.
- **European Union** — drones must meet EASA class marking and registration.

## Practical checklist

- Identify the item category and whether it transmits radio frequencies.
- Check both the **carrier** prohibition list and the **destination** import rules.
- For controlled electronics, confirm any required certification (e.g. ANATEL, FCC, CE)
  before shipping.
